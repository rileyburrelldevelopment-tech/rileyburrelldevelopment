Frequently asked questions

What is EchoPath?

EchoPath is an accessible navigation app designed for blind and visually impaired users.

Does EchoPath require an account?

No. EchoPath does not currently require users to create an account or sign in.

Why does EchoPath need location permission?

EchoPath needs location permission so it can provide navigation and guidance features.

Is my location used for tracking?

No. EchoPath does not use precise location for tracking or advertising.

How do I report feedback during beta testing?

Use TestFlight feedback if you are testing through TestFlight, or email support with details of the issue.

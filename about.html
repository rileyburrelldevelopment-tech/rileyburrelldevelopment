About Riley Burrell Development

Riley Burrell Development is an independent development brand creating apps, games and digital experiences with accessibility, creativity and practical use at the centre.

Built from lived experience

EchoPath is being developed by Riley Burrell, a blind developer building tools that reflect real accessibility needs.

The goal is to create technology that is clear, useful and empowering for people who rely on accessible design every day.

What we value

  • Accessibility from the start
  • Privacy-conscious design
  • Clear, simple interfaces
  • Real feedback from users
  • Continuous improvement

Introducing EchoPath

EchoPath is an accessibility-first navigation app designed to help blind and visually impaired users travel with clearer guidance, saved places and useful safety tools.

Accessible navigation

Clear spoken guidance and a high-contrast interface designed around accessibility from the start.

Saved places

Save important locations and return to them quickly when you need guidance.

Privacy-first design

EchoPath uses location for app functionality and is designed so location data is not linked to your identity.

Our mission

Riley Burrell Development exists to create apps, games and digital tools that are accessible, practical and built with real-world use in mind.

EchoPath is the first major app in this journey, with more accessibility-focused improvements planned for the future.

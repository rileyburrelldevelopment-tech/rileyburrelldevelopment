Privacy Policy

EchoPath Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 June 2026

Overview

EchoPath is designed to help blind and visually impaired users navigate with greater confidence. Privacy is important to Riley Burrell Development, and EchoPath is designed to collect as little personal data as possible.

Data EchoPath uses

EchoPath uses precise location data to provide app functionality, including navigation, guidance, distance information and saved place features.

Location data

Precise location is used for navigation and app functionality. EchoPath does not use precise location for advertising or tracking purposes.

Data linked to you

EchoPath does not currently require user accounts or sign-in. Location data is not linked to your identity by Riley Burrell Development.

Saved places

If you save places in EchoPath, that information is intended to support app functionality. Saved places are used so the app can help you return to important locations.

Third-party services

EchoPath uses Apple technologies such as Core Location and MapKit to support navigation features. Apple may process data according to Apple's own privacy policies and system services.

Tracking and advertising

EchoPath does not use precise location for tracking. EchoPath does not currently display third-party advertising.

Children

EchoPath is not specifically directed at children. The app is designed as an accessibility and navigation tool.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact Riley Burrell Development at rileyburrelldevelopment@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as EchoPath develops. Any changes will be posted on this page.
